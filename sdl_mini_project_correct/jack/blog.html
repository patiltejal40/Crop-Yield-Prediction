Brief Introduction

Why to use crop yield predictor ?

Due to the impact of climate change in India, most of the agricultural crops are being badly affected in terms of their performance over a period of the last two decades. Predicting the crop in advance of its harvest would help the policy makers and farmers for taking appropriate measures for marketing and storage. It attempts to solve the issue by building a prototype of an interactive prediction system. The results of the prediction will be made available to the farmer. This project helps to predict the suitable crop according to the season ,area and production of crop in that respective area. It will provide accurate prediction and estimation of farming parameters. The proposed system aims at predicting the crop yield by learning the past data of the farming land.  By using factors such as area, season, crop year and other entities the system builds a predicting model using machine learning techniques. To achieve higher yield of crops by predicting the production of respective crops. To obtain better quality and higher yield of crop. This project will help the farmers to know which crop will be better before cultivating onto the agricultural field and thus help them to make the appropriate decisions by using algorithms such as Random Forest and Linear Regression. It facilitates web-based information flow. Using crop prediction can lead to greater efficiencies in agricultural production. This system will help farmers who do not have sufficient resources to get adequate information
